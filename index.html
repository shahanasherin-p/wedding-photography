Less Posing, More Fun!

One of the best things about having a city wedding or civil ceremony is that you can make the most of the urban backdrop. Whether it's murals and grafitti popping in the background or iconic buildings, adopt a candidly cool pose for some delightfully louche results. This one works just as well in country towns too, which can be full of character. The key is to interact with your surroundings, and the people you meet - the more real the better...

Many couples get a few posed shots in the bag (or don't bother at all!) and then move swiftly on to fun, alternative, shots that are more candid and relaxed in style. Your photographer will most likely check out your venue in advance and suss out fun spots or unique focal points, so chat to them about their ideas.
